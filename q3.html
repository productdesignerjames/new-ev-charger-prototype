Which cable set up suits you best?

Need a hand deciding?

A fixed cable (tethered) charger has its cable permanently attached, so you just pull it out and plug it in, while with a removable-cable (untethered) charger is simply a socket-you attach your own cable each time and can store it away to keep the unit neat; whichever option you choose, check if your new EV already includes a charging cable before buying one.

Change your mind? No problem - Tell us any time before your installation date and we will update your order
